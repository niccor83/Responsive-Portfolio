About Me

I am an aspiring full stack web developer. I am originally from Logan, Utah but I currently live in Davis County. I am married and have three children. I enjoy watching movies, playing golf, riding motorcycles, mountain biking, and spending time with my family. I have a bachelors degree in Management Information System from Utah State University, go Aggies! I currently work for Intermountain Healthcare as an Application Systems Technical Analyst. During my schooling I took a few classes in web development and really enjoyed them, ever since I have wanted to learn to code. I have always enjoyed art, solving problems, being creative, and building things. I feel like web development gives me the opportunity to put all those things I enjoy into practice as well as giving me an opportunity to build upon my education. I am very excited to see where this journey will take me.
